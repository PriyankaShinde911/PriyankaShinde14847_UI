MY OFFICE TIMELINES

At Mastek, we recognise your potential to be an excellent self-managed professional.
Our flextime policy empowers you with the ability to choose your work timings and plan
holidays without approvals. You can alter your workday start and finish time as long as
it doesn?t interfere with a ?core? period of the day when you are required to be at work.
In a week, you will be required to complete 43 hours and 45 min of work (at your own pace).
The total number of leaves allocated for a year is 26. And oh! You also get a complimentary
off on your Birthday.
